# Circuit Theory

## Core Concept

> Circuit theory is the foundation for studying circuits and its components.  It focuses on mathematical tools, models, and analytical techniques to understand and predict the behavior of electric circuits.

## Explanation

There are several assumptions that are made in circuit theory, but three main ones:

1. Electrical effects happen *instantaneously* throughout a system
2. The net charge of every component in the system is zero
3. There is no magnetic coupling between components in a system
