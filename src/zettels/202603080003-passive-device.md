# Passive Device

## Core Concept

> In electronics, a passive device is a component in a circuit that does not depend on an external power source to function. They do not provide power gain or amplify signals, but instead consume, store, or dissipate energy.

## Explanation

This type of device does not increase signal gain, does not require external power, and either consumes, stores, or dissipates energy.

## Example

- Resistors
- Capacitors
- Inductors
- Fuses
- Transformers
