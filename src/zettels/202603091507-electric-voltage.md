# Electric Voltage

## Core Concept

> Voltage is the *electrical potential* difference between two points that forces [[202603091507-electric-charge|charge]] to drift (as in to create a [[202603091507-electric-current|current]]).

Strictly speaking, voltage is the energy per unit charge required to move charge through an electromagnetic field.  And it is measured in Volts.
$$
V = \frac{W}{Q}
$$

Framed in the context of Ohm's Law, voltage is current multiplied by resistance:

$$
V = I \times R
$$
