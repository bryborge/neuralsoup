# Electric Current

## Core Concept

> Electric current is the rate at which [[202603091507-electric-charge|charge]] moves.  Many books describe this as "flow" but it's more like a "drift".

The following is the mathematical description of current as "the change charge per unit time."
$$
I = \frac{\Delta Q}{\Delta t}
$$

The much more common model for current is in terms of Ohm's Law -- "voltage over resistance":

$$
I = \frac{V}{R}
$$
