# Electric Power

## Core Concept

> Electrical power is the rate at which work is done to transfer or convert into another form of energy.  It's unit is the Watt ($W$).

$$
\begin{aligned}
P &= V \times I \\\\
P &= (IR) \times I = I^2R \\\\
P &= \frac{V^2}{R}
\end{aligned}
$$
