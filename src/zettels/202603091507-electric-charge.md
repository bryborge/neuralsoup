# Electric Charge

## Core Concept

> Electric charge is the "stuff" that gets moved around in a circuit. This isn't factually true, but it is a very useful way to think about it, because the literal definition is pretty abstract.

**Literal Definition**

Charge is the fundamental physical property of matter that causes subatomic particles to experience a force when placed in an electromagnetic field.  Charge itself is intrinsic to matter, similar to mass.

The smallest quantity of charge is $1.602 \times 10^{-19}$ Coulombs, and the *net* charge is an integer multiple of that value.
