# Coefficient Matrix

## Core Concept

> A coefficient matrix is an array of numbers, all of which are the coefficients of the variables in a set of linear equations.

$$
\begin{aligned}
 {\color{red}a_{11}}x_1 + {\color{red}a_{12}}x_2 + {\color{red}a_{13}}x_3 &= b_1 \\
 {\color{red}a_{21}}x_1 + {\color{red}a_{22}}x_2 + {\color{red}a_{23}}x_3 &= b_2 \\
 {\color{red}a_{31}}x_1 + {\color{red}a_{32}}x_2 + {\color{red}a_{33}}x_3 &= b_3
\end{aligned}

\qquad \longrightarrow \qquad

\begin{bmatrix}
 {\color{red}a_{11}} & {\color{red}a_{12}} & {\color{red}a_{13}} \\
 {\color{red}a_{21}} & {\color{red}a_{22}} & {\color{red}a_{23}} \\
 {\color{red}a_{31}} & {\color{red}a_{32}} & {\color{red}a_{33}}
\end{bmatrix}

\begin{bmatrix} x_1 \\
 x_2 \\
 x_3 \end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix} b_1 \\
 b_2 \\
 b_3 \end{bmatrix}
$$
