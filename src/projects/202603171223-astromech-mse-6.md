# Astromech MSE-6 (Mouse Droid)

## Project Brief

### What

MSE-6 (commonly referred to as "Mouse Droid") is a much smaller, less complex astromech to build.  It's a great way to get started designing and building droids, and it is much cheaper (financially and time commitment).

### Why

- Bragging rights
- Make people smile
	- Events, meetups, children's hospitals, etc
