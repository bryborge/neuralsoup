# Electrical Circuits I: Map of Content

## Executive Summary

> Introduces students to basic circuit elements and circuit analysis techniques.

This course was taken at [Portland Community College (PCC)](https://www.pcc.edu/).

## Major Classifications in Electrical Engineering

- Communication Systems
- Computer Systems
- Control Systems
- Power Systems
- Signal-Processing Systems

## Core Concepts

- [[202603082228-circuit-theory|Circuit Theory]] - The bedrock for learning about electrical circuits and devices.
- [[202603080003-passive-device|Passive Devices]] - Electrical devices that do not require external power.
- [[202603080003-active-device|Active Devices]] - Electrical devices that do require external power.
- [[202603082327-circuit-branch|Branch]] - A trace that contains only one circuit element.
- [[202603082327-circuit-node|Node]] - Electrical connection points where wires and/or components come together.
	- [[202603082335-ordinary-node|Ordinary Node]] - An electrical connection point between 2 (and only 2) devices.
	- [[202603082335-extraordinary-node|Extraordinary Node]] - An electrical connection point between 3 or more devices.
- [[202603082335-circuit-path|Path]] - A continuous sequence of branches.
- [[202603082328-circuit-loop|Loop]] - A closed path that starts and ends at the same point.
- [[202603082330-circuit-mesh|Mesh]] -  A loop that contains no other loops.
- [[202603082330-series-circuit|Series]] -  Circuit elements connected end-to-end, sharing the same current.
- [[202603082330-parallel-circuit|Parallel]] - Circuit elements connected at either end to the same nodes, sharing the same voltage.
- [[202603091507-electric-charge|Charge]] - The "stuff" that moves around in a circuit.
- [[202603091507-electric-current|Current]] - The rate that charge is moving.
- [[202603091507-electric-voltage|Voltage]] - The intensity with which the charge is moving.
- [[202603091507-electric-power|Power]] - The amount of electrical energy necessary to move charge.

## Circuit Journal

- [[202603051802-voltage-divider|Voltage Divider]] - A simple passive circuit that reduces high input voltage to a low output voltage.
