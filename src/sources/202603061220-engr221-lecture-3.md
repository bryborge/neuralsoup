## Key Terms & Definitions

- **Wye (Y) Transformation:** A method to convert a delta (Δ) resistor network into an equivalent wye (Y) network.
- **Delta (Δ) Transformation:** A method to convert a wye (Y) resistor network into an equivalent delta (Δ) network.
- **Balanced Circuit:** A circuit where all resistors in the delta or wye configuration have equal values.
- **Wheatstone Bridge:** A circuit consisting of four resistors and a DC power supply, used to measure unknown resistances.
- **Balanced Wheatstone Bridge:** Condition where the ratio of two resistor pairs is equal, resulting in zero voltage between nodes A and B.
- **Unbalanced Wheatstone Bridge:** Condition where the resistor ratios are unequal, resulting in a measurable voltage between nodes A and B.
