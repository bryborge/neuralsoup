## Key Terms & Definitions

- **Node-Voltage Analysis:** A method for determining the voltage at different points (nodes) in a circuit.
- **Mesh Current Analysis:** A technique to find current in circuit loops (meshes).
- **Linear Circuit:** A circuit where output is directly proportional to input.
- **Superposition:** Technique where the total response in a linear system is the sum of responses from each independent source.
- **Thevenin Equivalent:** A simplified circuit with a single voltage source and series resistance representing a more complex network.
- **Norton Equivalent:** A simplified circuit with a single current source in parallel with a resistance.
- **Open Circuit Voltage (Vth):** The voltage across terminals when no load is connected.
- **Short Circuit Current (In):** The current through terminals when they are shorted.
- **Maximum Power Transfer Theorem:** States that maximum power is delivered to the load when load resistance equals Thevenin resistance.
