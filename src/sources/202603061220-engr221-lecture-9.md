## Key Terms & Definitions

- **Time Constant (τ):** The time required for the current or voltage in an RL circuit to change significantly, calculated as τ = L/R.
- **Transient Response:** The behavior of the circuit as it transitions from one state to another, typically within 5τ.
- **Steady-State Response:** The behavior of the circuit after 5τ, when changes have effectively ceased.
- **Natural Response:** The response of the circuit with no external sources, determined by initial conditions and circuit parameters.
- **RL Circuit:** A circuit consisting of resistors (R) and inductors (L).
